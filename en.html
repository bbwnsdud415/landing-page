GIL-GAMJA STORY

On the shores of Gwangalli Beach, a simple idea blossomed into Busan's most iconic "must-try" snack. Beyond just fried potatoes, we packed the thrill of the ocean and an unmatched chewiness into every single cup.

Addictive Texture

The golden ratio found through hundreds of tests. Crispy first bite, followed by a surprisingly chewy finish.

Instagrammable Visuals

A stylish package that looks like a photo shoot against the Gwangandaegyo Bridge. Taste meets style.

Daily Fresh Guarantee

We select only the freshest potatoes and knead them every morning. Healthy street food made with heart.
